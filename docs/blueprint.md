# **App Name**: Crimson Coals

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog Display: A rich landing page showcasing available shisha products with visually appealing layouts.
- Product Search: Enables users to efficiently search for products using keywords, categories, or filters.
- Detailed Product Pages: Individual pages for each product, displaying comprehensive descriptions, high-resolution images, and pricing.
- Shopping Cart Management: Allows users to add, remove, and update quantities of items within their shopping cart before checkout.
- Admin Product Management: A secure administrative interface for adding, editing, and deleting shisha products.
- AI Product Description Assistant: An AI-powered tool for administrators to automatically generate rich product descriptions based on minimal input.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, vibrant crimson (#DF2030) to evoke passion and sophistication, contrasting with the dark theme.
- Background color: A very dark, almost black charcoal with a subtle hint of the crimson hue (#1D1616) to provide a sophisticated and luxurious backdrop.
- Accent color: An elegant, aged gold (#D1A347) to highlight key elements, call-to-actions, and important information, adding a touch of luxury.
- Headlines will use 'Playfair' (serif) for an elegant, high-end feel. Body text will use 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for excellent readability and a modern touch.
- Utilize minimalist, clean line icons with a subtle golden tint or glow, aligning with the sophisticated dark theme and gold accents.
- A grid-based layout with ample negative space to highlight product imagery, maintaining a clean and premium feel despite a rich product catalog. Full-width hero sections will introduce featured items.
- Subtle, fluid animations for navigation transitions, elegant hover effects on product cards, and smooth content loading to enhance the user experience without being distracting.
